Sarvodaya Hospital & Research Centre Sector 8 Faridabad (Delhi-NCR)

Sarvodaya Hospital & Research Centre is amongst the finest medical institutions in Faridabad. Spread across 4.25 acres, the hospital has 300 Beds, 65 ICU Beds, 6 Operation Theatres, and a floor base d Cath Lab. It delivers care-based super specialty services in Cardiology, Cardio-Thoracic & Vascular Surgery, Cancer Care, Neurosciences, Minimal Invasive Surgery, Gastroenterology & GI Surgery, Orthopedics & Joint Replacement, Urology, and Nephrology. Sarvodaya has established a culture of ‘Cure+Care’ and has built a team that upholds this mantra for the hospital at all times. Amongst the many ideologies of helping patients with utmost kindness, one that is common in all Sarvodayans is ‘Service with a smile’.
